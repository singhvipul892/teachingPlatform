Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Singh Sir ("we", "our", or "the app") is a maths teaching platform that provides video lessons and PDF resources. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your first name, last name, email address, mobile number, and password. When you log in, we use your username and password.

Session data: After login, we store your authentication token, user ID, first name, last name, and email on your device to keep you signed in and personalise the app.

Usage data: When you download PDFs, we store metadata (user ID, video ID, PDF ID, and local file path) on your device to manage downloads.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate you and maintain your session
  • Provide access to video lessons and PDF resources
  • Track which PDFs you have downloaded for offline access

3. Data Storage

On your device: Session data and PDF metadata are stored locally using secure storage mechanisms.

On our servers: Your account information is stored on our backend servers at teacherplatform.duckdns.org to enable authentication and account management.

4. Third-Party Services

Backend API: We use our own backend service to handle signup, login, and content delivery. Data is transmitted over HTTPS.

YouTube: Video lessons are embedded from YouTube (youtube-nocookie.com). When you watch videos, YouTube may collect data according to its own privacy policy. We do not control YouTube's data practices.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share data only with our backend service as needed to operate the app.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions

To exercise these rights or for any privacy concerns, contact us using the details in the Contact section.

7. Security

We use HTTPS for all network communication. Passwords are stored in hashed form on our servers. Session tokens are stored securely on your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at the email or contact details provided in the app's Contact Us section.
